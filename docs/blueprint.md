# **App Name**: Growing Up Muslim Events V2

## Core Features:

- Smooth Scrolling: One-page design with smooth scrolling to different sections.
- Event Listing: Displays upcoming events with key details and a link to registration.
- Speaker Spotlighting: Showcase speakers with their photo.
- Community Engagement: Includes a newsletter signup and links to community channels like WhatsApp.
- Volunteer Sign-Up: Collects form data for volunteer sign-ups via form submission.
- Secure Contributions: Links to Stripe for secure contributions to support GUM's work.
- Sentiment Analysis: AI tool assesses user message sentiments from the contact form, routing urgent or negative feedback for immediate attention.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light pastel green (#BFD7B5) to evoke a sense of growth and tranquility, aligning with GUM's mission. The muted tone ensures it complements a Swiss-like design.
- Very light gray (#F0F0F0), close to white, to maintain ample whitespace and a minimalist aesthetic. The light tone ensures the focus remains on content and interactive elements.
- Muted orange (#E5C07B), analogous to green but with a warm contrast. The more saturated tone provides contrast. The washed-out color ensures visual interest without overwhelming the minimalist style.
- Clean, legible sans-serif fonts to emphasize readability and align with a modern Swiss-like design. Use varying font weights and sizes to create a clear visual hierarchy.
- Minimalist and consistent icons for key sections and CTAs to enhance usability and maintain a clean aesthetic. Icons will primarily be monochrome (dark gray or primary color).
- Grid-based layout with ample whitespace and large, impactful images to showcase speakers and events. The layout will ensure responsiveness across all devices.
- Subtle animations on scrolling and hover effects to add a touch of interactivity without detracting from the minimalist design. Transitions should be smooth and unobtrusive.
